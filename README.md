# MSACCMGR
## Microsoft Account Manager with Generative AI Professional Certificate
### Professional Certificate - 5 course series

Advance your career as an Account Manager by mastering AI-driven insights and customer engagement with Microsoft Copilot. The MSFT Account Manager with Copilot Professional Certificate is designed for professionals who are ready to elevate their roles or transition into modern account management. This program combines Copilot and generative AI tools to enhance productivity, streamline workflows, and foster stronger customer relationships across various industries.

You’ll develop skills in data-driven decision-making, ethical negotiation, and real-world problem-solving while building a strong portfolio through hands-on projects. By the end, you’ll be prepared for roles such as Advanced Account Manager or Strategic Partner Manager, equipped with practical expertise in tools like Microsoft Office and CRM platforms.

A basic level of computer proficiency and experience with the Microsoft Suite is recommended. Strategic thinking, adaptability, and a passion for technology will support your success. Completing the program earns a certificate that demonstrates your ability to leverage AI for strategic account management and opens up new career opportunities.
