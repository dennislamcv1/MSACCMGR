--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1A. RISK INDICATOR SUMMARY (120–150 words)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Analysis of CRM health data across all three strategic accounts reveals a
converging set of retention risks that demand immediate attention. InnovateCorp
Manufacturing presents the most acute danger: a measurable usage plateau over
the past quarter, compounded by the departure of our primary internal champion,
has eroded relationship continuity and left renewal momentum stalled. Satisfaction
scores have dipped below the 7.0 threshold, and competitive outreach has been
detected. GrowthDynamics Retail, while operationally healthy, faces an external
threat — at least two competitors are actively pursuing the account, exploiting
gaps in our multi-location scalability narrative during a critical growth phase.
FinancialFirst Services signals declining satisfaction driven by unmet compliance
workflow needs, a risk amplified by new regulatory demands that our current
configuration does not fully address. Across all three accounts, stakeholder
alignment gaps and unresolved functional pain points represent the highest-
priority churn drivers requiring immediate, coordinated action.
