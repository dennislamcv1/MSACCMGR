----------------------------------------------------------------

During the first 30 days managing the Precision Marketing Solutions account,
my priority is to establish a reliable, transparent, and well-documented
communication rhythm with Marcus and his team. I will conduct weekly
check-ins via video call or phone, supplemented by mid-week email updates

Marcus full visibility into our progress from day one.

----------------------------------------------------------------

1. WEEK 1 | Welcome Call
   Type:      Introductory video call
   Purpose:   Formally introduce myself, confirm account goals, and set
              expectations for the engagement going forward.
   Admin Action: Create a new client profile in the CRM; log call notes;
                 file signed NDA and onboarding documents in the client
                 folder; send a follow-up summary email within 24 hours.

2. WEEK 1 | Welcome Email
   Type:      Written communication (email)
   Purpose:   Provide Marcus with a written record of our introduction,
              key contacts, and initial next steps.
   Admin Action: Save a copy of the sent email to the client's digital
                 folder; add Marcus's preferences and communication style
                 notes to his CRM contact record.

3. WEEK 2 | Needs Assessment Meeting
   Type:      Structured video call with shared agenda
   Purpose:   Conduct a deep-dive review of Precision Marketing Solutions'
              current challenges, priorities, and success metrics.
   Admin Action: Prepare and distribute a pre-meeting agenda 48 hours
                 in advance; document all insights in a Needs Assessment
                 Summary; update CRM with findings and tag action items.

4. WEEK 3 | Mid-Month Progress Check-In
   Type:      Phone call
   Purpose:   Review progress on agreed action items, address any early
              concerns, and confirm alignment on priorities.
   Admin Action: Pull and review the action-item tracker prior to the
                 call; update task statuses in real time during the
                 conversation; file updated tracker in the client folder
                 post-call.

5. WEEK 3 | Resource & Document Delivery
   Type:      Email with attachments
   Purpose:   Deliver relevant resources, service overviews, or materials
              discussed during the Needs Assessment Meeting.
   Admin Action: Organize documents in clearly labeled folders; log
                 delivery in the CRM; track whether materials were
                 acknowledged or require follow-up.

6. WEEK 4 | End-of-Month Review Call
   Type:      Formal video call with recap agenda
   Purpose:   Evaluate the first 30 days, celebrate early wins, identify
              gaps, and collaboratively set goals for the next 60 days.
   Admin Action: Prepare a written 30-Day Recap Report in advance;
                 present and file the report in the client folder; update
                 the CRM with revised goals, key decisions, and agreed
                 next steps for Month 2.
